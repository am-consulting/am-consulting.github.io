【参考】http://www.stat.go.jp/data/roudou/120424/index.htm

\[ \begin{align} \frac{\Delta Y}{Y} & = k-c \times \Delta u \\ \\ Y & \textrm{ is actual output.} \\ c & \textrm{ is the factor relating changes in unemployment to changes in output.}\\ \Delta Y & \textrm{ is the change in actual output from one year to the next.}\\ \Delta u & \textrm{ is the change in actual unemployment from one year to the next.}\\ k & \textrm{ is the average annual growth rate of full-employment output.} \end{align} \]
